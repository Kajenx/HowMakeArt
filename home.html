Welcome!


I hope that after going through these tutorials you will feel comfortable drawing from your imagination without the need for references. I know the frustration of looking at professional art and wondering how the artists managed to pull complete pictures out of their heads. After many years of struggling, I have come to a place where I feel comfortable drawing realistic figures and landscapes without reference, and I believe anyone can use the same formulas that I do to construct imagery with good results.

The site is currently a work in progress, so if you follow me on my facebook page, you can keep track of when I upload new material. Good luck, and be patient with yourself!
